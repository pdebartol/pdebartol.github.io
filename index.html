Piersilvio De Bartolomeis

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at ETH Zürich, advised by Fanny Yang, and part of the Institute for Machine Learning. I was previously an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Science in Seattle, where I worked on machine learning and experimentation to select the best product offers for customers. Before that, I was a visiting graduate student at Harvard University, hosted by Issa Dahabreh in the CAUSALab.

My current research focuses on trial augmentation - a new line of work that improves the efficiency of clinical trials by safely integrating external data and flexible machine learning models into trial design and analysis.
